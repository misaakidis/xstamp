Welcome to XStamp, the revolutionary digital stamp duty service designed for the Republic of Cyprus.

Say goodbye to time-consuming trips to the courthouse or notarization offices, because with XStamp, you can now easily and securely stamp your legal agreements from the comfort of your own home. Our platform harnesses the power of the XRP blockchain to offer a seamless, efficient, and cost-effective solution for citizens seeking to validate their agreements.

XStamp allows you to save both time and money by providing a user-friendly interface for digitally stamping your agreements. Whether you're entering into contracts, leases, or any other legal arrangements, our platform ensures that your documents are securely stored on the XRP blockchain, preventing any unauthorized modifications and enabling easy verification. You can make payments conveniently using your bank card or the XRP Euro stablecoin, making the entire process hassle-free.

With XStamp, the future of digital stamp duty services has arrived, putting control and convenience back in the hands of Cyprus citizens. Stamp your agreements and validate previously stamped ones with confidence, all while embracing the benefits of blockchain technology.
