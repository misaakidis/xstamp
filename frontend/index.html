Welcome to XStamp, the revolutionary digital stamp duty service designed for the Republic of Cyprus.

XStamp offers a convenient alternative to traditional methods of legal document stamping. Skip the time-consuming visits to courthouses or notarization offices, and instead, securely stamp your agreements from the comfort of your home. Our platform utilizes the XRP blockchain, ensuring a smooth and cost-effective solution for citizens looking to validate their agreements.

XStamp streamlines the process, saving you both time and money. It provides an easy-to-use interface for digitally stamping various legal documents, from contracts to leases. These digital stamps are securely stored on the XRP blockchain, preventing unauthorized alterations and facilitating simple verification. Payments can be made conveniently using your bank card or the XRP Euro stablecoin.

With XStamp, the future of digital stamp duty services is here, offering control and convenience to the people of Cyprus. You can confidently stamp new agreements and validate previously stamped ones, all while benefiting from the advantages of blockchain technology.
